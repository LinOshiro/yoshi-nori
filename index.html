Daily Routines

  • Yoshi and Nori are both potty trained and can hold their bladder up to 8 hours if needed. (*Best is every 4-6 hours)
  • They wake up around 8/8:30 A.M. and always need to potty when they wake up and before they go to bed at night.
  • They both get a dental treat after they potty in the morning.(In Whimzee Box) Yoshi gets the little one, Nori gets the larger one.
  • They eat twice a day. Breakfast between 10:00-11:00 A.M. and then Dinner between 6:00-7:00 P.M.
  • They can have treats a few times a day but please supervise them when they eat any food.
  • The Dogs always go outside to potty after eating a meal.

Yoshi

Yoshi is a feisty 13 year old Silky-Terrier. He can be very protective of his home and family. Mostly though he is very sweet, playful and loves to give kisses. His favorite game is fetch.

Yoshi has a sensitive stomach and should not be given a lot of treats even though he will always ask for one. Just tell him no! üòÇ

Food: On a plate each meal he gets about 1/2 cup of fresh food (In the fridge). In our bathroom is his dry food bowl, he will graze and eat it when he wants. Please just keep it filled. Under Kyle's cabinet is his food container and scoop. Please also change and rinse this water bowl daily.

Vitamins to be given with dinner.

Vitamins:

  • 1/2 Senior dog vitamin -daily
  • Joint Chew: 1 full chew broken in half *every other day.
  • Salmon Oil (In fridge door): 1 pump daily.

Nori

Nori is our sweet and gentle giant who is 4. She is a Great Dane mix who thinks she is as tiny as her brother, but not as brave. She is very timid and afraid of loud noises. She will follow you everywhere trying to play or give big snuggles. Please give her lots of love when you visit her!

Food: In her bowl please mix 1 scoop of her dry food (In the pantry) with 1 cup of fresh food (In the fridge). Mix it well together please or else she will eat too quickly and vomit. üòÖ

Please rinse and change water bowl daily. Please wash her bowl with hot water and soap if it gets gross.

Vitamins to be given with dinner.

Vitamins:

  • 2 whole vitamins -daily
  • 1 joint chew -daily
  • Salmon Oil (In fridge door): 3 pumps daily.
